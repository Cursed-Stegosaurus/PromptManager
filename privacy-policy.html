Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Extension: Prompt Cortex

Contact: nacheen@mac.com

Overview

Prompt Cortex does not collect, store, or share personal information. The extension runs on your device. Data stays on your device unless a Chrome permission sends data to a site you choose.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data.
  • No browsing history.
  • No cookies from you.
  • No unique identifiers.
  • No analytics.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions it needs to work. The extension uses permissions to run features on your device. The extension does not use permissions to collect data for the developer.

Local Processing

All processing happens in the browser unless a feature must call an external site that you use. If a feature needs to reach a site, the request goes directly from your browser to that site.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not send data to third-party services owned by the developer. If you sign in to a site, that site handles your data under its policy.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not for children under 13. The extension does not collect personal data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will change the date at the top when we do. Your continued use means you accept the new policy.

Contact

If you have questions, email nacheen@mac.com.
